Support

Chumlord is a small side project. The fastest way to get help, report a bug, or request a feature is to open an issue on GitHub.

github.com/morelandjs/chumlord-site/issues

Common questions

How do I add a friend?

Tap the + button in the top corner. You can pick from your contacts or add someone manually.

How do I record a catchup?

Swipe a friend to the side from the main list. The list will re-rank to show who's most overdue next.

Where is my data stored?

On your iPhone. There's no backend, no account, and nothing is transmitted off the device. See the privacy page.

How do I delete a friend or my data?

Delete a friend in the app to remove that friend and their catchups. Delete the app to remove everything.
