# Chumlord Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-05_

Chumlord is a personal friend-catchup tracker. Your data stays on your device.

## What we collect

**Nothing is sent off your device.** Chumlord does not have a backend. It does not log, transmit, or share any of the information below with us or any third party.

The app stores the following on-device, in your iPhone's local app storage:

- Friends you add: name, optional phone number, optional photo, the catchup cadence you assign, optional status emoji, optional next-catchup date.
- Catchup events you log: the date you record for each catchup.
- Frequency tiers you create or edit.

## Permissions Chumlord asks for

Each permission is requested only the moment you tap the action that needs it. Chumlord never reads or imports anything in the background.

- **Contacts** — used only when you tap "Add from contacts" to pick a friend's name and phone number. Contacts are not copied or transmitted.
- **Photos** — used only when you tap "Change photo" to pick an image to attach to a friend. The chosen image is stored locally on your device.
- **Camera** — used only if you choose to snap a custom photo for a friend.

## Sharing and tracking

Chumlord performs no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no sharing of data. There are no SDKs that send data anywhere.

## Deleting your data

Delete a friend in the app to remove that friend and all their catchups. Delete the app to remove every Chumlord record from the device.
